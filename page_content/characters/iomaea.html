Background

Kael hails from Ravenguard, a war-scarred fortress city in the north. Raised in the barracks, he climbed the ranks to become the youngest commander in the city's history.

Combat Style

Kael fights with a longsword imbued with fire runes. His battle stance focuses on aggressive counters and shield bashes.

Signature Moves

  • Flame Edge
  • Crimson Parry
  • Iron Resolve

Notable Events

- Fought in the Siege of Hollowmere.
- Was saved by Aeris during the Battle of Ashplain.

Personality

Stubborn but fiercely loyal. While he lacks political tact, his sense of honor inspires those around him.
