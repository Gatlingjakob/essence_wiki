Background

Aeris was born in the twilight groves of Elenor, a secluded elven kingdom untouched by human wars. Gifted with innate magical talent, she was chosen by the Archmage's Circle at a young age.

Abilities

Aeris specializes in elemental magic, particularly wind and lightning. Her signature spell, "Tempest Chain", can incapacitate entire squads.

Known Spells

  • Tempest Chain
  • Windwalk
  • Arcane Shield

Allies

Aeris maintains a strong alliance with Kael, the knight of Ravenguard, and shares a tense rivalry with the necromancer Valthor.
